MER — Marketing Efficiency Ratio

Revenue divided by marketing spend. Indicates how much top-line revenue each dollar of marketing generates. Does not account for costs.

CMAM — Contribution Margin After Marketing

Revenue minus cost of goods and marketing, expressed as a percentage of revenue. Measures profitability after marketing investment.

CAC — Customer Acquisition Cost

Marketing spend divided by customers acquired. Measures the average cost to acquire one customer. Optional — enter customers acquired to enable.
